A once-a-day movie-guessing game. Six actors revealed in reverse billing order — obscure character actor first, headliner last. Six points; every reveal and every wrong guess costs one. Name the film before you run out. Below: all six screens in the primary palette, a color study in the alternate palette, and a component & token spec.

How to play
One mystery movie. Every day. Everyone gets the same one.
1
Six clues, reverse billing. The cast reveals from the most obscure character actor up to the headliner.
2
You start with six points. Revealing a clue costs one. A wrong guess costs one.
3
Guess any time. Name the film right and your remaining points are your score. The fewer clues, the better.
