Cluevie

A new movie quiz every day. Name the film from six actor clues — revealed hardest first, the most obscure character actor before the headliner.

How to play

  • You start with 6 points.
  • Clues are six actors from the movie, revealed in reverse billing order.
  • Each clue you reveal and each wrong guess costs 1 point.
  • Guess the movie before you run out. One new puzzle daily.
